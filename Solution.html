Aerosols or aerosols are colloidal mixtures consisting of aerosolic solid or liquid particles dispersed in the

air or in other gases. But the current includes droplets, liquids, or both, dispersed in gases.

Aerosols contain small particles (between 10–100 micrometers in diameter) suspended

in the air in a solid state and a liquid state. It originates from natural sources, such as volcanoes

, forest fires, seawater droplets, dust, smoke, erosion of the soil surface by wind, etc.
